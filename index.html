Hotel plan

Current working plan: Antibes/Juan-les-Pins to start, Nice for the wedding block and airport logistics, then Sezz in Saint‑Tropez.

Aug 31 – Sept 2

Hôtel Belles Rives

Waterfront Juan-les-Pins / Cap d’Antibes base with private beach, Fitzgerald-era glamour and easy access to Old Antibes.

  • Book sea-view room if possible
  • Use for arrival beach/reset day
  • Great for La Passagère or Belles Rives beach

Open hotel site

Sept 2 – 4 + final night

Palais de la Méditerranée

Nice Promenade hotel with sea views and a rare indoor/outdoor pool setup — best wedding-logistics fallback if Vence is full.

  • Good for Vence driver pickup
  • Easy airport access
  • Book sea-view or deluxe sea-view

Open hotel site

Sept 4 – 6

Sezz Saint‑Tropez

Stylish 5-star non-beachfront St‑Tropez base with a strong pool scene, easy shuttle/taxi access and better pricing than Cheval Blanc.

  • Keep Loulou/Gigi for beach days
  • Prebook taxis after late dinners
  • Good match for a fun-car arrival

Open hotel site
